Struggling to start tasks, stay focused, or even feel where the time goes?  
This ADHD-friendly timeboxing tool is built specifically for people who deal with:

- Time blindness  
- Mental overload  
- Task paralysis  

Instead of expecting you to “just focus,” it guides you step-by-step into action.

---

## 🚀 Features

### ⚡ Smart Task Suggestions
Get instant, ADHD-friendly task ideas so you don’t get stuck deciding what to do.

### ✍️ Custom Task Input
Add your own tasks easily — no rigid systems or friction.

### ⏱ Visual Timeboxing Timer
A clear countdown that creates urgency without stress.

### 🔋 Energy-Based Planning
Choose your energy level (High / Low) and get tasks that match how you feel.

### 📅 Calendar Integration - Coming Soon!
Quickly add time blocks to:
- Google Calendar  
- Outlook Calendar  
- iCal  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. **Select your energy level**  
   Work *with* your brain, not against it.

2. **Choose a timeframe**  
   Short, manageable blocks (10–30 minutes).

3. **Pick a suggested task (or add your own)**  
   No overthinking required.

4. **Start the timer**  
   Stay anchored with a clear countdown.

5. **Complete the block**  
   Build momentum naturally.

---

## 💡 Why This Tool Exists

ADHD isn’t a lack of motivation — it’s a gap between **intention and action**.

This tool helps close that gap by:
- Reducing decision fatigue  
- Making tasks feel smaller and easier to start  
- Creating structure without overwhelm  
- Turning time into something visible and tangible
